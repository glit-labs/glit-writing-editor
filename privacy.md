# Privacy Policy — Glit: Writing Editor

Last updated: 2026-06-25

Glit: Writing Editor ("Glit", "we") is a writing app for web novel creators. This policy explains how your data is handled across all versions of the app (macOS, Windows, iPhone, and iPad).

## No data collected by us
We do not operate our own servers and do not collect, store, or share any personal information. Glit contains no analytics, advertising, or tracking software. No account or registration is required to use the app.

## Your files are stored on your device
All projects and files you create or edit are stored locally on your device.

## Cloud storage (optional)
Glit never requires cloud storage and works fully offline. If you choose to use it, how it works depends on your platform:

**On macOS and Windows:** You sync your work by installing the cloud provider's own desktop app (such as Google Drive, Dropbox, or OneDrive), which creates a synced folder on your computer. Glit simply opens and saves files in that folder like any other local folder. Glit itself does not connect to the cloud service or access your account.

**On iPhone and iPad:** Glit connects directly to Google Drive, Dropbox, or Microsoft OneDrive through their official APIs to sync the files you choose. You sign in through the service's secure OAuth flow; your authorization tokens are stored securely on your device and are never sent to us. Glit accesses only the files needed to sync your work, and never your account password.

## Third-party services
When you use a cloud service (on any platform), that service is governed by its own privacy policy:

- Google (Google Sign-In, Google Drive): https://policies.google.com/privacy
- Dropbox: https://www.dropbox.com/privacy
- Microsoft (OneDrive): https://privacy.microsoft.com/privacystatement

## Children's privacy
Glit is not directed to children and does not knowingly collect personal information from anyone.
