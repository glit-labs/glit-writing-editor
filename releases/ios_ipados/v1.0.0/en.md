# Glit for iOS Release Notes — v1.0.0

**Release date:** In App Store review  
**First public release (iPhone · iPad)**

---

## Overview

Glit arrives on iPhone and iPad — a focused Markdown writing app for web novelists. Write each novel in its own dedicated project, organized by episodes and chapters.

---

## Key Features

### Project-based Organization

Write each novel or series in its own dedicated project, organized by episodes and chapters.

### Distraction-free Markdown Editor

A distraction-free Markdown editor keeps you in the flow — optimized for both iPhone and iPad.

### Reader Preview

Preview your manuscript exactly as readers will see it.

### Cloud Sync (free)

Your manuscripts stay on your device and sync your way with Google Drive, Dropbox, or OneDrive. Notes, folders, and cloud sync are always free.

### Free / Glit Pro

- **Free**: one work project.
- **Glit Pro** (one-time purchase): unlimited work projects, so every story has its own space.
- No subscription. No account required to start writing.

---

## Requirements

- iOS / iPadOS 17.0 or later
